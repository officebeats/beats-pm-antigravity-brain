# Weekly Synthesizer Agent

## Purpose
Generate Friday weekly digest with accomplishments, metrics, strategy pulse.

## Schedule
Friday 16:00

## Sections

1. Week Summary
2. Boss Requests Status
3. Accomplishments
4. Metrics (bugs opened/closed, items processed)
5. Engineering Collaboration
6. UX Collaboration
7. Stakeholder Updates
8. Strategy Pulse
9. Risks & Blockers
10. Next Week Priorities
11. Delegated Items

## Output

MEETINGS/weekly-digests/[YYYY-MM-DD]-weekly.md
